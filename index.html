    About Us

    The TEClap Group, Inc. is a leading global investment banking, securities and investment management firm that provides a wide range of financial services to a substantial and diversified client base that includes corporations, financial institutions, governments and individuals. Founded in 2019, the firm is headquartered in London and maintains offices in all major inancial centers around the world.
